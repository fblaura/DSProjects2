Tableu - public profile

15/8/2021 One-minute read

In this second post I’ll explain about Tableu Public:

Tableau is a Business Intelligence tool that allows you to analyze, visualize and share large volumes of information in a fast, flexible and friendly way. Any user just drags the fields of interest to achieve the crossing of information and obtain an attractive visualization. It also offers the opportunity to create new fields, formulas and dynamic relationships.

By following the link below it is possible to find different analysis publications using Tableu-public. The analyses have been made based on public datasets from job related topics to business topics.
